## Official SEGA / RGG Studio Apparel

SEGA produces official branded clothing through its online store and at events — T-shirts, hoodies, and accessories with game-specific graphic prints across the series.

[🛒 Search official Yakuza apparel on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=yakuza+like+a+dragon+shirt+official&tag=tetsuakira-21)

**SEGA Official Shop (JP):** [sega.jp/toys](https://sega.jp/toys) — the most direct source for current official apparel. Requires a forwarding service for international orders.

## Notable Collaboration Lines

### UNIQLO UT Collaborations

UNIQLO's UT graphic T-shirt line has featured Yakuza / Like a Dragon in multiple collections. Sold internationally through UNIQLO stores and online — the most accessible officially-licensed clothing available outside Japan.

Previous UT collections covered *Yakuza 0*, *Yakuza: Like a Dragon*, and *Infinite Wealth* designs. UNIQLO announces UT collaborations seasonally; the Yakuza series returns periodically.

[🛒 UNIQLO UT on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=uniqlo+ut+yakuza&tag=tetsuakira-21) | [UNIQLO official site](https://www.uniqlo.com)

## Event Merchandise

Tokyo Game Show and RGG Studio fan events produce exclusive runs not available through standard retail:

- Branded tote bags and pouches
- Enamel pins and badge sets (character portraits, the Dragon logo)
- Collaboration clothing with event branding

Event exclusives appear on [Mercari Japan](https://www.mercari.com/jp/) shortly after events. Use a proxy service like **Buyee** to purchase internationally.

## Sizing Notes

Japanese official apparel uses Japanese size standards — typically one size smaller than European/American equivalents. A Japanese L is usually a European/American M. Check size charts before ordering.
