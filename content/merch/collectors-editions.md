The Yakuza series has produced collector's editions and special releases primarily for the Japanese market, with a smaller number of Western premium editions from *Yakuza 6* onwards. The Japanese releases are typically more elaborate and more expensive — both at launch and on the secondhand market.

---

## Notable Japanese Collector's Editions

### Yakuza 0 — Premium Edition
Released alongside the standard edition in Japan. Included a physical art book (the design archive edition), a set of in-game item codes, and special packaging with gold-foil finish. The art book component is the main draw — it's the most detailed production archive released for a Yakuza 0 volume.

### Yakuza 6: The Song of Life — Essence of Art Edition (Japan)
One of the more ambitious collector's packages in the series. Included:
- A physical art book
- An in-game item pack
- Special outer packaging
The Onomichi-set cover art made this a visually distinctive release.

### Like a Dragon: Ishin! — Collector's Box (2023 Remake)
Included a replica of the Imōse accessory (a prop from the game), art cards, and additional in-game content. The replica item has become one of the more sought-after collector's pieces from recent releases.

### Like a Dragon: Infinite Wealth — Deluxe / Ultimate Editions
The most recent major release had tiered digital and physical editions. The physical Ultimate Edition (Japan and select Western markets) included a steelbook case and physical bonus content. Steelbook editions of this quality are worth picking up at launch — they're generally not reprinted.

---

## Western Collector's Editions

Western collector's editions for the series began in earnest with *Yakuza 6* and have continued through recent releases.

**Yakuza 6 — The Song of Life Essence of Art Edition (West)** — Western version of the Japan collector's edition, adjusted for market. Art book included. One of the few Western Yakuza collector's editions with a genuine physical art component.

**Judgment — Premium Edition** — Included a steelbook and in-game bonus content. Limited quantities; sold out quickly at launch in most Western markets.

**Like a Dragon: Infinite Wealth — Ultimate Edition** — The most recent major Western collector's release. Tiered physical content.

---

## What to Look For Secondhand

The most valuable pieces on the secondhand market:
1. **Japanese art book editions** — the design archive volumes, particularly *Yakuza 0* and the Dragon Engine Technical Design Book
2. **Steelbooks** — especially Judgment and Infinite Wealth, which had limited print runs
3. **Sealed Japanese editions** — sealed Japanese collector's editions of *Yakuza 0* and *Kiwami* from the period of the series' Western popularity surge

**Secondhand sources:** Mandarake, Mercari Japan, Yahoo Auctions JP (for Japanese editions); eBay for Western editions.

---

## Reproduction / Fake Warning

Premium Japanese game collector's editions are a target for reproductions. When buying sealed Japanese editions secondhand, particularly at prices that seem low for the item's rarity, verify the seller's rating and ask for specific photos of the item number on the spine. Authentic SEGA Japan releases have specific print quality on the box text that reproductions tend to get slightly wrong.
