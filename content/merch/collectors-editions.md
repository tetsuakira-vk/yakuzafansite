The series has produced collector's editions primarily for the Japanese market, with a smaller number of Western premium editions from *Yakuza 6* onwards.

---

## Notable Japanese Collector's Editions

### Yakuza 0 — Premium Edition
Included a physical design archive art book, in-game item codes, and gold-foil packaging. The art book is the main draw — the most detailed production archive released for the game.

[🛒 Search Yakuza 0 collector's edition on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=yakuza+0+collector+edition&tag=tetsuakira-21)

### Yakuza 6: The Song of Life — Essence of Art Edition
Included a physical art book, in-game item pack, and special outer packaging. The Onomichi-set cover art made this a visually distinctive release.

[🛒 Search Yakuza 6 Essence of Art on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=yakuza+6+essence+of+art+edition&tag=tetsuakira-21)

### Like a Dragon: Ishin! — Collector's Box (2023 Remake)
Included a replica of the Imōse accessory (a prop from the game), art cards, and additional in-game content. One of the more sought-after collector's pieces from recent releases.

[🛒 Search Like a Dragon Ishin collector's on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=like+a+dragon+ishin+collector&tag=tetsuakira-21)

### Like a Dragon: Infinite Wealth — Ultimate Edition
Steelbook case and physical bonus content. Steelbook editions are generally not reprinted — worth picking up at launch.

[🛒 Like a Dragon: Infinite Wealth on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=like+a+dragon+infinite+wealth+ultimate&tag=tetsuakira-21)

## Reproduction Warning

Premium Japanese game collector's editions are a target for reproductions. When buying sealed Japanese editions secondhand at prices that seem low for the item's rarity, verify the seller's rating and ask for specific photos. Authentic SEGA Japan releases have specific print quality on the box text that reproductions tend to get slightly wrong.
