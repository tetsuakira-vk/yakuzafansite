## Story

**Takayuki Yagami** was a promising defence attorney until a client he got acquitted went on to murder his girlfriend. Disgraced and expelled from the bar, Yagami reinvented himself as a private detective. He's good at it — too good.

A serial killer is targeting yakuza members, gouging out their eyes post-mortem. The investigation leads into the heart of a pharmaceutical conspiracy that has corrupted police, medicine, and the Tojo Clan alike. Yagami's legal mind and his network in Kamurocho's underworld make him uniquely positioned to unravel it — if he survives.

*Judgment* is a noir detective story set in the Yakuza universe, with a protagonist who approaches the same city from a completely different angle.

---

## Detective Mechanics

Where Kiryu fights his way through Kamurocho, Yagami investigates it:

- **Tailing and surveillance** — follow suspects without being spotted, using Yagami's knowledge of Kamurocho's layouts and cafes for cover
- **Drone investigation** — deploy a drone to reach otherwise inaccessible areas
- **Parkour traversal** — Yagami moves through the city more acrobatically than Kiryu, wall-running and climbing between locations
- **Crime scene analysis** — examine evidence, connect clues, and build cases

The detective systems are woven into the main story rather than bolted on, making Kamurocho feel genuinely investigable.

---

## Combat: EX-Actions and Dual Style

Yagami fights in two styles — **Crane** (crowd control, wide sweeping attacks) and **Tiger** (focused, single-target pressure). EX-Actions are cinematic finishing moves triggered by positioning. The system is faster and more evasion-focused than Kiryu's brawling.

---

## Kaito

**Masaharu Kaito**, Yagami's partner and former yakuza, is one of the best supporting characters RGG Studio has written. His loyalty, moral complexity, and relationship with Yagami are the emotional backbone of the game.

---

## Note on Availability

The PS4 version was briefly delisted due to a licensing dispute involving the Japanese celebrity who voices Yagami. The PS5/Xbox/PC versions remain available.
