## Story

*Yakuza Kiwami* retells the story of the original game — the case that defines Kazuma Kiryu. After taking the blame for the murder of Dojima Family patriarch Sohei Dojima (a crime committed by his sworn brother Akira Nishikiyama), Kiryu serves ten years in prison. He walks out in 2005 to find Kamurocho changed, the Tojo Clan in chaos, and ten billion yen missing.

The missing money is connected to Yumi Sawamura, a woman from Kiryu's past, and a young orphan girl named Haruka. What follows is a story of betrayal, brotherhood, and the cost of loyalty.

Kiwami is more than a port — it rebuilds the game with modernised mechanics, re-shot cutscenes, and a mountain of new content not in the original, including expanded backstory for Nishikiyama and entirely new substories.

---

## Majima Everywhere

The defining new addition to Kiwami. Goro Majima obsessively hunts Kiryu across Kamurocho in increasingly unhinged ways — as a street punk, a police officer, a chef, a zombie, a cabaret girl. Beyond being hilarious, the system is how Kiryu relearns his Dragon Style, locked at the start to reflect his decade of imprisonment.

It is one of the most beloved additions in the series' history and fundamentally changes how you move through the city.

---

## Differences from the Original

- Dragon Engine predecessor replaces the dated PS2 engine
- Nishikiyama chapters expanded into a meaningful parallel narrative
- Majima Everywhere system added
- New substories added, many tying into *Yakuza 0*
- *Yakuza 0* save data unlocks bonus content

## Where to Play

Available on **PS4**, **PS5** (backwards compatible), **Xbox**, and **PC via Steam**. Frequently discounted. Part of the *Yakuza: Like a Dragon — Legendary Hero Edition* bundle on some storefronts.
