## What Is This?

*Fist of the North Star: Lost Paradise* is an RGG Studio game in everything but protagonist and setting. The engine, structure, tone, side content format, hostess clubs, and minigame sensibility are pure Yakuza. The combat is pure *Hokuto no Ken* — Kenshiro targets pressure points that make enemies explode in showers of blood several seconds later, while the game's internal logic cheerfully refers to this as "vital point strikes."

It is as entertaining as that sounds.

---

## Story

Set in an alternate interpretation of the *Fist of the North Star* world, Kenshiro searches for his missing fiancée Yuria and arrives at **Eden** — a walled city in the wasteland trying to maintain civilisation in the post-nuclear world. Behind Eden's ordered surface: criminal factions, power struggles, and secrets about Yuria's whereabouts.

The game creates space for both the existing *Hokuto no Ken* mythology and original RGG Studio storytelling.

---

## The Kamurocho-Eden Parallel

Eden is essentially Kamurocho in apocalypse cosplay — a dense urban grid with bars, hostess clubs (now post-apocalyptic cabarets), shops, and street encounters. The structural familiarity is intentional and delightful for Yakuza fans who know every RGG Studio city rhythm.

---

## Minigames

The minigame roster is a highlight: running an apocalyptic bar, kart racing through the wasteland, a full bartending simulator, and baseball using post-apocalyptic improvised equipment. Each is committed to the bit.

---

## Recommended For

Yakuza fans curious about *Fist of the North Star*, and *Fist of the North Star* fans who want a full modern game. Knowledge of one franchise enriches the other, but neither is required.

The game is PS4 exclusive and has not been ported.
