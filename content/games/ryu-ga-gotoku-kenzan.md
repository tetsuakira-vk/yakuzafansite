## Story

*Ryu ga Gotoku Kenzan!* is set in Kyoto, 1605, during the early Edo period — a time of shifting power following the Battle of Sekigahara. The protagonist is **Miyamoto Musashi**, the legendary swordsman of Japanese history, rendered in the image of Kazuma Kiryu.

Musashi arrives in Kyoto searching for a man named **Sasaki Kojiro** — the rival whose duel with Musashi is one of the most famous in Japanese history — and finds himself entangled in a conspiracy involving the shogunate and rival samurai factions.

The supporting cast maps series regulars onto historical roles: **Goro Majima** appears as **Sasaki Kojiro**, Kiryu's nemesis-turned-something-more, while other familiar faces take on period-appropriate counterparts.

---

## Significance

*Kenzan!* is the prototype for *Like a Dragon: Ishin!* — both in concept (Yakuza cast in historical Japan) and in structure (open-world city exploration, side content, samurai combat). The later game *Ishin!* covers a different period (1860s Bakumatsu) and is considered its spiritual successor.

RGG Studio experimented in *Kenzan!* with a more grounded, slower-paced narrative than the mainline games, leaning into the melancholy of the historical setting. The result was critically well-received in Japan.

---

## How to Play

*Kenzan!* has never received a Western release or an official fan localisation. The most comprehensive option for Western players is the partial fan-translation patch that covers much of the main story text, available through the RGG modding community.

A full remake in the style of *Like a Dragon: Ishin!* has been requested by Western fans since Ishin's 2023 release, but no announcement has been made.

---

## Note

*Kenzan!* is the first game in the series' internal chronology (by fictional historical date), predating even *Yakuza 0* by over three centuries of in-universe time.
