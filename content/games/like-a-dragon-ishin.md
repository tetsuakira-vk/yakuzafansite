## Story

*Like a Dragon: Ishin!* takes the cast of the Yakuza series and transplants them into the **Bakumatsu** — the tumultuous final years of the Edo period, as samurai clans and pro-imperial factions fought over Japan's future.

The protagonist is **Ryoma Sakamoto**, one of the real historical figures of the Meiji Restoration, but rendered in the image of Kazuma Kiryu. His quest: to find the mysterious **Tenma** group responsible for his adoptive father's murder, and to uncover the truth about the ruling Shinsengumi — a legendary ronin militia.

The supporting cast maps series regulars onto historical roles. **Goro Majima** appears as Ryoma's sworn brother **Takechi Hanpeita**, a fierce idealist. **Daigo Dojima** plays **Toyo Itou**, a political strategist. It's a cast reunion wearing period costume, and RGG Studio commits to the premise completely.

## Combat

*Ishin!* uses a hybrid combat system blending action combat with samurai weapons and firearms:

- **Swordsman** — fast katana strikes and parries
- **Gunman** — mid-range pistol combat
- **Wild Dancer** — acrobatic sword + pistol dual-style
- **Brawler** — unarmed combat, the Kiryu staple

The combat feels more tactical than the brawler entries, with deflect timing and stagger mechanics rewarding precision.

## Why It Matters for Western Fans

This game was never localised in its original 2014 form. Western fans played for years with fan translations or without. The 2023 remake's full localisation was a significant event for the community — finally bringing a beloved Japan-only entry to a global audience with a full graphical overhaul.
