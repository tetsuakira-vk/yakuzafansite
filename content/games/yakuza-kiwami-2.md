## Story

One year after the events of *Yakuza Kiwami*, a power vacuum in the Tojo Clan is pushing Japan toward a full-scale yakuza war. The Omi Alliance, based in Osaka, is mobilising — and at the head of their military operations is **Ryuji Goda**, self-proclaimed Dragon of Kansai, son of the Omi Alliance chairman.

Kiryu is pulled back in to broker peace. What he finds in Osaka is a mirror of himself — a man of towering strength and principle on the opposite side of a conflict neither of them started. Their eventual confrontation is among the most celebrated in the series.

Kiwami 2 also features a full **Majima Campaign** — a standalone chapter following Majima's actions during the main story timeline, previously only hinted at.

---

## Dragon Engine Debut

*Kiwami 2* is the first remake to use the **Dragon Engine**, the same technology behind *Yakuza 6*. This means:
- Seamless transitions into combat with no loading screens
- Dramatically improved character models and lighting
- Interactive environments (bottles, chairs, signs all become weapons naturally)
- More fluid, physics-based combat

The visual leap over Kiwami 1 is significant.

## Key Characters

- **Ryuji Goda** — Dragon of Kansai, primary antagonist and one of the series' most compelling rivals
- **Kaoru Sayama** — Detective assigned to the Omi case, strong-willed and formidable
- **Goro Majima** — Features in his own playable chapter
- **Daigo Dojima** — The Tojo Clan's nominated sixth chairman, a recurring figure going forward
