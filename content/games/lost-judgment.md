## Story

A man is convicted of a sexual harassment charge. On the same day, a murder occurs in a locked room — and the convicted man has an unbreakable alibi because he was in police custody. When the death is ruled a suicide, Yagami is hired to find the truth.

The investigation leads to **Seiryo High School** in Yokohama, where a student bullying network operates under the noses of compliant teachers, and where the line between victim and perpetrator keeps shifting. *Lost Judgment* is the darkest story RGG Studio has told — a case that asks whether the legal system can ever deliver justice, and whether breaking the law to protect the vulnerable is ever right.

---

## Dual City Structure

*Lost Judgment* splits between **Kamurocho** (Tokyo) and **Ijincho** (Yokohama), the setting from *Yakuza: Like a Dragon*. The two cities give the game a sense of geographic scope, and the Yokohama sections benefit from being the Ichiban game's city repurposed for Yagami's investigation style.

---

## School Stories

A major new content system: Yagami goes undercover at Seiryo High, joining school clubs (dance, boxing, robotics, motorcycle, etc.) and building relationships with students. Each club is a full minigame system. The school setting is used seriously — bullying, teacher complicity, and student despair are treated with weight.

---

## Combat Expanded

*Lost Judgment* adds a third style — **Snake** — which focuses on grapples, joint locks, and counters. It's the most technical of the three and rewards patience. The parkour and traversal systems are also expanded.

---

## The Kaito Files DLC

A 4–5 hour expansion following Kaito solo, investigating a case from his past. A significant emotional addition to his character. Recommended after completing the main game.
