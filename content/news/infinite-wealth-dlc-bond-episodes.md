*Like a Dragon: Infinite Wealth* launched in January 2024 and RGG Studio have been rolling out post-launch content across the following months. Here's what's been released and what it adds.

## Bond Episodes

Bond Episodes are story DLC chapters focused on individual party members — expanding on backstories and relationships that the main game established. They take roughly 1–2 hours each and are played separately from the main story.

These are optional but add genuine character depth to members whose in-game arcs were limited by the main story's scope. For players who found themselves invested in specific party members, they're worth it.

## Premium Adventure

Premium Adventure is the game's post-credits free-roam mode, available after completing the main story. It lets players continue exploring Honolulu, complete any remaining substories, and access end-game content without returning to the main campaign. This is available in the base game but is accessed differently depending on version.

## Palekana Dungeon

Additional end-game dungeon content for players who cleared the main story and want more challenge. The dungeon scales with party level and includes new enemy configurations.

## Is the DLC Worth It?

For players who completed the main story and want more time with the characters — yes, the Bond Episodes in particular are a worthwhile addition. The base game is complete without them; they're supplementary rather than essential.

The current editions of Infinite Wealth bundle varying amounts of this content. Check the Steam page for the most current package details.
