SEGA and Ryu Ga Gotoku Studio have detailed the post-launch content schedule for *Like a Dragon: Infinite Wealth*, and there's more than the usual cosmetic drip. Here's what's confirmed.

## Bond Episodes

Bond Episodes are the game's character-specific side story content, and the post-launch slate adds new episodes for several party members. These aren't filler — if the base-game Bond Episodes are the benchmark, each one is a 30–60 minute story that recontextualises the character's arc.

Confirmed additions include episodes for **Chitose Fujinomiya** and an extended chapter for **Kazuma Kiryu** dealing directly with the aftermath of his cancer storyline. The Kiryu episode in particular has been described by the Japanese community as one of the most emotionally impactful pieces of content in the entire franchise.

## Palekana Dungeon Expansion

The post-launch dungeon expansion deepens the Aloha State Job system with new floors, enemy types, and exclusive gear. Endgame grinders will want to revisit Palekana once the update drops.

## Costume Packs

Three costume packs are scheduled, including one with callbacks to classic Yakuza game outfits — early screenshots show Ichiban in the iconic white suit from *Yakuza: Like a Dragon's* end section.

## Dates

RGG Studio has not committed to a firm release calendar beyond "2024," consistent with their usual approach. We'll update this post as dates are confirmed.
