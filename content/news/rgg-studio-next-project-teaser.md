Ryu Ga Gotoku Studio posted a short teaser on their official social channels last week — a black screen, the sound of a city at night, and the words **「次の舞台へ」** (*"To the next stage"*). That's it. That's the whole thing. And naturally, the community has not stopped talking about it since.

## What the Teaser Shows

Almost nothing. Which, honestly, is very on-brand for RGG Studio, who have a history of announcing games with minimal fanfare and then dropping a full trailer a month later.

The audio contains what sounds like rain on pavement, a distant pachinko parlor, and — if you boost the gain — what several Reddit users claim is a brief snatch of a new musical motif. Fan analyses have noted the audio processing sounds closer to the dense urban texture of Kamurocho than any Hawaii-adjacent soundscape.

## Community Theories

**Judgment 3 / Lost Judgment sequel**: The Judgment sub-series has been in limbo since the licensing dispute over Takuya Kimura's likeness stalled international releases. A sequel was widely expected before the franchise went quiet.

**Kiryu-focused continuation**: Given the emotional weight of Kiryu's arc in *Infinite Wealth*, some fans expect a final Kiryu chapter before RGG Studio fully transitions to Ichiban as the primary protagonist.

**New protagonist, new city**: The most adventurous theory — a new lead, possibly set in a new city (Osaka? Nagoya? International?) — would fit RGG Studio's pattern of reinvention.

## Our Take

The audio texture leans toward a Japanese city setting. Whether that's a Judgment continuation or a new Kiryu story, the "next stage" phrasing suggests a significant new chapter rather than a spin-off. We'll keep tracking this closely.
