*Like a Dragon: Infinite Wealth* launched worldwide on January 26, 2024, on PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S, Xbox One, and PC via Steam.

The game is the longest, most expensive, and most content-heavy entry in the series' history. It brings Ichiban Kasuga back as the primary protagonist and Kazuma Kiryu back for what amounts to his final arc — dealing with a terminal diagnosis across a story that takes both characters to Honolulu, Hawaii.

## Critical Reception

Review scores landed around **88 on Metacritic**, making it one of the best-reviewed entries in the series. Critics highlighted the emotional weight of Kiryu's storyline and the sheer volume of content — the game has been reported to clock over 100 hours for completionists.

## What's New

- **Hawaii setting** — Honolulu's Kalani Island (a fictionalised Oahu) is one of the largest open-world environments RGG Studio has built
- **Sujimon** — a full creature-collecting minigame in the vein of Pokémon, with its own evolution chains and battle system
- **Dual protagonists** — Kiryu and Ichiban each have their own story threads that converge
- **Expanded party system** — larger party roster than *Yakuza: Like a Dragon*, with new party members including Chitose Fujinomiya and Eric Tomizawa

## Kiryu's Arc

The most-discussed aspect of the game is Kiryu's storyline, which deals with his terminal cancer diagnosis and constitutes a retrospective look at everything the character has been through across twenty years of the series. It has been described by multiple reviewers as some of the finest writing in the franchise.
