## About This Site

The **Ryu Ga Gotoku Archive** is a fan-run resource dedicated to the *Yakuza / Like a Dragon* franchise developed by Ryu Ga Gotoku Studio (SEGA).

This site is not affiliated with SEGA or Ryu Ga Gotoku Studio. All game titles, characters, and related media are the property of their respective owners.

### What We Cover

We aim to be the most comprehensive English-language fan resource for the series, including:

- **Game Entries** — mainline games, remasters, and spin-offs from *Yakuza 0* through *Like a Dragon: Infinite Wealth*
- **Characters** — detailed profiles for the heroes, rivals, and scene-stealers that define the series
- **Guides** — gameplay tips, substory guides, minigame breakdowns, and completion checklists
- **News** — announcements, trailers, and community coverage
