## Background

Yu Nanba was a nurse. He lost his job and license after taking the fall for a colleague who caused a patient's death through negligence — a decision that cost him his career, his apartment, and eventually his reasons to keep trying. By the time Ichiban meets him, he's been living rough in Ijincho for years.

He is not particularly interested in being saved. He is not particularly interested in being Ichiban's friend. He agrees to help anyway, because some part of him can't quite stop caring, no matter how much effort he puts into pretending otherwise.

---

## Character

Nanba is the counterweight to Ichiban's relentless optimism — a man who has every reason to believe the world doesn't work the way Ichiban thinks it does, and who is proved partially wrong. His arc in *Like a Dragon* is about recovering the possibility of caring: not the belief that everything will work out, but the willingness to act as if it matters even when it might not.

His medical background gives the party its healer, and his street-level knowledge of Ijincho gives it credibility with the district's homeless network.

---

## Combat Role

Nanba's job class is **Homeless Guy** by default — which becomes **Host** and later other classes as he progresses. His skillset uses pigeons (his companions during his homeless years) as projectiles and support tools. It is unexpected. It works.

---

## Infinite Wealth

Nanba returns in *Infinite Wealth*, now working with Ichiban's operations and having rebuilt some of the life he lost. His continued presence in the party reflects how thoroughly he was changed by the events of *Like a Dragon* — he stayed.
