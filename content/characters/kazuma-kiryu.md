## Background

Kazuma Kiryu grew up in the Sunflower Orphanage in Tokyo alongside Akira Nishikiyama and Yumi Sawamura. Both boys were inducted into the Dojima Family of the Tojo Clan as teenagers — a life they chose because it was the only world they understood.

Kiryu's early life, and the specific event that forged his reputation, is told in *Yakuza 0*: his role in the Empty Lot affair, his refusal to be made a scapegoat, and the emergence of the fighting philosophy that would follow him for decades.

---

## Character

Kiryu operates by a code he never articulates but never violates: protect the weak, take responsibility for what you've done, and do not run from consequences. He is not naive about the world — he has lived in its worst corners — but he refuses to let that reality make him smaller.

He is terrible at normal conversation. He is completely incapable of asking for help. He raises an orphan girl as a daughter while insisting he is not her father. He is beloved by the entire series' audience because the gap between how stoic he presents and how much he obviously cares about everyone is enormous and never quite closes.

---

## The Dragon Style

Kiryu's fighting style — **Dragon of Dojima** — is the series' signature combat system. A balanced brawler that emphasises heat actions: powerful, context-sensitive finishing moves triggered by positioning, environment, and charged energy. In *Yakuza 0*, Kiryu learns four styles (Brawler, Rush, Beast, Dragon). By the later games, Dragon Style alone covers most situations.

His fighting is described by enemies as unreadable — neither purely aggressive nor purely defensive, adapting completely to whatever is in front of him.

## Key Story Beats (Spoilers)

- **Y0**: Framed for murder by the Dojima Family. Fights to clear his name. Learns who Majima is before either becomes famous.
- **Kiwami**: Takes the fall for Nishikiyama's murder of Dojima. Serves ten years. Returns to find Kamurocho changed and his past unravelled.
- **Y6**: Intended as his final chapter. Discovers Haruka has a son. Fights to protect Haruka's new family and makes the decision to disappear.
- **Gaiden**: Living under the alias Joryu, doing covert work for the Daidoji Faction. Receives cancer diagnosis.
- **Infinite Wealth**: Confronts his mortality in Hawaii alongside Ichiban Kasuga. His story is given a second, deeper ending.

---

## Legacy

Kiryu is the most iconic character in RGG Studio's history and one of the most distinctive protagonists in modern gaming. He is not the loudest character in the room, not the most articulate, not the most strategic. He is simply the person who, when everyone else has made their calculation about what can be done, does it anyway.
